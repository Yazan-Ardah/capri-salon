About Us

More Than a Haircut — A Full Experience

At Capri Salon Barbershop, we believe grooming is an art form. Our team of skilled barbers and specialists is dedicated to delivering top-tier results in a welcoming, professional atmosphere.

We serve every client with care — whether you walk in, call for a home visit, or need us at a hospital bedside. No appointment? No problem. Surprises are always on the way.

  • Open 10 AM – 12 AM daily (late night 1:30 AM)
  • Dedicated kids' barber specialist
  • VIP private suite for premium experience
